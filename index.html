We are a two team crew who mostly work on projects involving robotics and electronics. Our first robot was called MOSS, hence the name MOSS robotics.

-----------------------------------------------

THE ORIGINAL ROBOT

The original robot is completely 3d printed using an Ultimaker original printer. An arduino controls the kinematics and basic functionality of the robot and a raspberry pi 3 runs a simple web interface. An artificial muscle design is implemented to mimic the way in which humans walk.
Video: https://youtu.be/K94D-TpBQpI

-----------------------------------------------

NEW ARTIFICIAL MUSCLES

We are also looking into new designs for the artificial muscle part of the robot. Flexible parts are necessary to package them into a single product. Possible pathways would be linear motors(induction and piezo) or smaller pulleys.
